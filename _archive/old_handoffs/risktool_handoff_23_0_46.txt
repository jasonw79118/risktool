RISKTOOL HANDOFF — PHASE 23.0.46
Date: 04.29.2026

Objective:
Move reporting and rollups from subjective/scored output toward a scenario-level, range-based Monte Carlo review engine.

Implemented:
1. Added scenario-level Monte Carlo engine for saved Single and Complex scenarios.
2. Uses Option A: probability and impact ranges. Current UI maps existing likelihood and hard/soft cost range fields into triangular probability and impact distributions; future phase should expose explicit probability min / most likely / max fields.
3. Complex scenarios aggregate component simulations by iteration, so scenario risk is the sum of component risk rather than a separate arbitrary area rollup.
4. Report page now includes Scenario Review & Hot Product Ranking:
   - ranks saved Single and Complex scenarios by residual expected loss ($)
   - groups scenario results into Area / Product Family hot spots
   - includes ranking charts for scenario risk and hot products
   - includes management concern language, top driver, P95, P99, and 5-year exposure
5. Outcome tables are available per scenario from the review table through View Outcomes and CSV.
6. Save Review to Workspace writes TXT and JSON to the reports folder when workspace folder access is connected; otherwise it downloads the report.
7. Version bumped to 23.0.46 and cache reference updated to app_23_0_46.js.
