COMPLEX SCENARIO DIRECTION
The intended modeling hierarchy is:
Product/Service/Area → Scenario → Risks / Insurance / Hard Facts / Mitigation / Accepted Risk → Overall Scenario Components

Interpretation:
- Product/Service/Area is the product-level grouping layer.
- A product/service/area may contain multiple scenarios.
- Each scenario may contain multiple risk items.
- Each scenario also carries its own insurance, hard facts/evidence, mitigation factors, and accepted-risk governance record.
- The Overall Scenario Components table is the rollup/index and belongs at the bottom.
