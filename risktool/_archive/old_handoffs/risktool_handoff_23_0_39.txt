OBJECTIVE
Refactor Complex Scenario Builder language and flow toward the intended hierarchy:
Area / Product Family → Area Components → selected component workspace → component-specific risk items, evidence, mitigation, insurance, accepted risk → area rollup.

1. Renamed Product Section Entry to Area / Product Family Setup.
2. Renamed Product Section Table to Area Rollup Summary.
3. Renamed Overall Scenario Components to Area Components.

5. Renamed Scenario Name to Component Name and Add Scenario to Save Component.
6. Updated labels/help text so components such as Loan Statements, Loan Disclosures, Loan Rates, Loan Transactions, Escrow, and Collections are treated as child components under a broader area such as Loans.
7. Updated area rollup calculation text and calculation basis so it considers component risk items across saved components plus the current workspace, evidence count, mitigation status, insurance offset, and exposure estimates.
8. Bumped visible version and cache references to 23.0.39.
