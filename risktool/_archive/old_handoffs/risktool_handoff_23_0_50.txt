RISKTOOL HANDOFF — PHASE 23.0.50
Date: 04.29.2026

OBJECTIVE
Separate portfolio-wide reporting from individual scenario review.

CHANGES INCLUDED
1. Added Portfolio Report menu/view.
   - Runs all saved Single and Complex scenarios from saved scenario storage.
   - Ranks by Average Component Risk.
   - Also displays Total Exposure for dollar materiality.
   - Includes Monte Carlo iteration selector: 100, 1,000, 10,000, 100,000.
   - Includes ranking chart and total exposure chart.
   - Includes per-scenario management recommendation narrative.
   - Includes View Outcomes and Export CSV actions per scenario.

2. Renamed existing Reports menu behavior to Scenario Review.
   - Scenario Review is intentionally for individual scenario drilldown.
   - Portfolio Report is the all-scenarios review engine.

3. Monte Carlo reporting updates.
   - Portfolio Report runs Monte Carlo for every saved scenario.
   - Uses range-based triangular sampling for hard cost and soft-cost ranges.
   - Shows P50/P90/P95/P99, expected loss, residual exposure, and volatility.

4. Version/cache update.
   - index.html references app_23_0_50.js?v=23.0.50.
   - app.js and app_23_0_50.js include Phase 23.0.50 logic.
   - Visible dashboard/sidebar version updated to 23.0.50.

TEST PLAN
1. Upload/replace files.
2. Clear browser cache or hard refresh.
3. Save at least two scenarios: one Single and one Complex.
4. Open Portfolio Report.
5. Confirm all saved scenarios appear.
6. Confirm ranking is by Average Component Risk and Total Exposure also shows.
7. Change Monte Carlo iterations and rerun report.
8. Click View Outcomes and Export CSV for a scenario.
9. Open Scenario Review and confirm it remains a single-scenario drilldown.

NOTES
The prior Reports page was tied to active/current scenario. This phase adds a separate all-scenarios Portfolio Report so the two concepts are no longer mixed.
