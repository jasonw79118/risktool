RISKTOOL HANDOFF — PHASE 23.0.47
Date: 04.29.2026

OBJECTIVE
Correct the Complex Scenario rollup and replace the prior score/rating behavior with a rankable, examiner-explainable average component residual risk value. Add Scenario Review reporting across saved Single and Complex scenarios with auto-run Monte Carlo outputs.

KEY CHANGE 1 — CALCULATED AREA ROLLUP SCORE
- The field now displays average component residual expected loss.
- It no longer shows a 1/5 score or old residual score text.
- Formula:
  Component Residual Risk = Expected Loss - Mitigation Reduction - Insurance Offset
  Area Rollup Score = Average(Component Residual Risk across saved components)
- Display format:
  $X avg component residual risk | N components | Status

KEY CHANGE 2 — COMPONENT RISK LOGIC
- Risk item expected loss uses probability × dollar impact.
- If probability/impact ranges exist, those are used.
- If ranges do not exist, defaults are inferred from available score/severity data.
- Mitigation reduces expected loss by effectiveness.
- Insurance offsets residual risk after deductibles/retentions where available.

KEY CHANGE 3 — SCENARIO REVIEW + HOT PRODUCT RANKING REPORT
- Added report card on Reports view.
- Reviews saved Single and Complex scenarios.
- Ranks scenarios by average residual expected loss.
- Complex scenario ranking uses average residual risk across components.
- Includes:
  - Scenario ranking graph
  - Average residual risk
  - P95 and P99 Monte Carlo outputs
  - Time horizon exposure: 1 year, 3 years, 5 years
  - Top driver
  - Management concern narrative
  - View Outcomes button
  - CSV export button

KEY CHANGE 4 — MONTE CARLO AUTO-RUN
- Monte Carlo runs automatically when the Scenario Review report is generated.
- Outcomes are bound to each report row.
- View Outcomes previews the first 25 rows.
- CSV exports the scenario outcome table for examiner/auditor support.
